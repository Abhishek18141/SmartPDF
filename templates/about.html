Smart PDF App - Project Overview

Transform your PDF documents into professional PowerPoint presentations with the power of AI

Smart PDF App is an innovative Flask-based web application that revolutionizes how we create presentations. By leveraging Google's Gemini AI technology, it automatically extracts meaningful content from PDFs and generates structured, professional slide presentations with intelligent summarization and visual integration.
